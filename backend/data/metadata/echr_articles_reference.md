# European Convention on Human Rights — Articles 1–19

This reference lists the first 19 articles of the ECHR, which are the substantive rights
relevant to ECtHR case law used in this project.

| Article | Title | Description |
|---------|-------|-------------|
| Art. 1  | Obligation to respect human rights | States must secure Convention rights to everyone within their jurisdiction |
| Art. 2  | Right to life | Protects the right to life; state obligation to protect life by law |
| Art. 3  | Prohibition of torture | Absolute prohibition of torture, inhuman or degrading treatment or punishment |
| Art. 4  | Prohibition of slavery and forced labour | No one shall be held in slavery, servitude, or required to perform forced labour |
| Art. 5  | Right to liberty and security | Protects personal liberty; no arbitrary detention; right to challenge detention |
| Art. 6  | Right to a fair trial | Fair and public hearing within reasonable time by independent tribunal; presumption of innocence |
| Art. 7  | No punishment without law | No criminal conviction for acts not criminal at time committed; no heavier penalty than applicable |
| Art. 8  | Right to respect for private and family life | Protects private life, family life, home, and correspondence |
| Art. 9  | Freedom of thought, conscience and religion | Freedom to change religion; manifest religion in worship, teaching, practice, observance |
| Art. 10 | Freedom of expression | Freedom to hold opinions, receive and impart information and ideas |
| Art. 11 | Freedom of assembly and association | Right to peaceful assembly and freedom of association, including trade unions |
| Art. 12 | Right to marry | Men and women of marriageable age have the right to marry and found a family |
| Art. 13 | Right to an effective remedy | Everyone whose Convention rights are violated has right to effective remedy before national authority |
| Art. 14 | Prohibition of discrimination | Convention rights secured without discrimination on any ground |
| Art. 15 | Derogation in time of emergency | States may derogate from obligations in time of war or public emergency (except Arts 2, 3, 4§1, 7) |
| Art. 16 | Restrictions on political activity of aliens | Permits restrictions on political activity of aliens under Arts 10, 11, 14 |
| Art. 17 | Prohibition of abuse of rights | No state, group, or person may destroy Convention rights or limit them beyond what Convention permits |
| Art. 18 | Limitation on use of restrictions on rights | Restrictions on rights shall not be applied for purposes other than those prescribed |
| Art. 19 | Establishment of the Court | Establishes the European Court of Human Rights to ensure observance of Convention obligations |

## Source
European Convention on Human Rights (as amended by Protocols Nos. 11, 14 and 15).
Full text: https://www.echr.coe.int/european-convention-on-human-rights
